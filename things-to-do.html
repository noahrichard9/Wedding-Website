Things to Do

Frederick's historic downtown is walkable, charming, and full of great food, shops, and sights - perfect for exploring before or after the celebration.

Eat & Drink

Restaurants

🍽️

Volt

228 N Market St

Chef Bryan Voltaggio's flagship restaurant, set inside a stunning Victorian mansion. Seasonal, upscale American cuisine - a special-occasion favourite and a Frederick landmark.

Brewer's Alley

124 N Market St

Frederick's original brewpub, housed in a beautifully restored 1890s city hall. Craft beers brewed on-site, hearty American food, and a lively atmosphere great for groups.

A cosy wine bar and restaurant right on Carroll Creek. Perfect for a pre-wedding dinner with curated small plates, an excellent wine list, and lovely creek views.

Morning & Afternoon

Coffee & Casual Bites

☕

Beans in the Belfry

122 W Patrick St

Coffee shop set inside a converted 1881 Baptist church - stained glass, soaring ceilings, and great espresso. One of Frederick's most unique and beloved spots.

Visit Website →
🫖

Frederick Coffee Co. & Café

1 E Church St

A welcoming neighbourhood café right in the heart of downtown. Locally roasted coffee, fresh pastries, and a relaxed vibe - a great spot to start the day.

Visit Website →
🍎

McCutcheon's Market

13 S Wisner St

A Frederick institution since 1938. Famous for apple butter, cider, and preserves made on-site. Grab a homemade snack, a jar to take home, or a fresh sandwich at the deli counter.

Visit Website →
December in Frederick

Carroll Creek Holiday Lights

Each December, Carroll Creek Linear Park is transformed with thousands of lights, decorated trees, and festive displays stretching along the waterway through downtown. It's free, walkable, and absolutely worth an evening stroll.

Shopping & Sights

📚

Curious Iguana

12 N Market St

Frederick's beloved independent bookshop, right on Market Street. Thoughtfully curated shelves, local author picks, and a warm atmosphere - a perfect rainy-day stop.

Visit Website →
🏛️

National Museum of Civil War Medicine

48 E Patrick St

A fascinating and unique museum telling the story of medical care during the Civil War. Frederick played a significant role as a hospital town - a great visit for history lovers.

Visit Website →
🛍️

Historic Downtown Shopping

N & S Market St + Patrick St

Stroll Frederick's walkable historic district - antique shops, boutiques, art galleries, and gift stores line the brick sidewalks. Perfect for an afternoon of relaxed browsing before the big day.
